
Branch is defined as part of a larger organization that might not be co-located with it. Branch is a kind of organizational sub-unit.
Division is defined as part of an organization, such as a line of business, that may have separate accounting and reporting requirements. Division is a kind of organizational sub-unit.
Joint venture is defined as legal entity that is formed between parties that pool their resources for the purpose of accomplishing a specific task but otherwise retain their distinct identities. Joint venture is a kind of legal entity.
Non-governmental organization is defined as not-for-profit organization that functions independently of government. Non-governmental organization is a kind of not for profit organization. It has objective some public purpose.
Not for profit organization is defined as organization that uses its surplus revenues to further achieve its purpose rather than distributing its surplus income to the organization's owners (directors, investors, and equivalents) as profit / dividends. Not for profit organization has synonyms non-profit organization. Not for profit organization is a kind of formal organization. It has objective some not for profit objective.
Organization covering agreement is defined as contract between the principals in a formal organization that specifies the relationship between the principals, and between the principals and the entity. Organization covering agreement is a kind of written contract.
Value-added tax identification number is defined as tax identifier that identifies a taxable person (business) or non-taxable legal entity for a consumption tax that is assessed incrementally, levied on the price of a product or service at each stage of production, distribution, and sale to the end consumer. Value-added tax identification number has synonyms VAT identification number VAT registration number. Value-added tax identification number is a kind of organization identifier, registry identifier, tax identifier.
