
Private student loan is defined as student loan that is not considered a government-backed / regulated loan. Private student loan is a kind of regulated student loan, student loan.
Regulated student loan is defined as student loan (1) whose lender is a government agency or instrumentality, and/or (2) that is treated uniquely due to tax regulations. Regulated student loan is a kind of student loan.
Student loan is defined as loan provided for the purposes of education, allowing students and parents/guardians to borrow money for college. Student loan is a kind of loan. It has some borrower that is that has identity some legally competent natural person.
