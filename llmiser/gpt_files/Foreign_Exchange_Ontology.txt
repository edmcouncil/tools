
Currency conversion service is defined as foreign exchange service involving the conversion of currency of one country or group of countries for another, typically, but not always, as a counter transaction. Currency conversion service is a kind of foreign exchange service.
Currency forward rate is defined as rate of exchange between two currencies for settlement at some future point in time, expressed as a premium on the spot rate. Currency forward rate is a kind of quoted exchange rate. It has settlement date only date.
Currency forward rate volatility is defined as measure of exchange rate fluctuation based on a range of projected values for exchange rates. Currency forward rate volatility is a kind of exchange rate volatility, implied volatility. It is volatility of only currency forward rate.
Currency spot buy rate is defined as indicative spot buying market rate as observed by the reporting source. Currency spot buy rate is a kind of currency spot rate.
Currency spot mid rate is defined as indicative middle market (mean of spot buying and selling) rate as observed by the reporting source. Currency spot mid rate is a kind of currency spot rate.
Currency spot rate is defined as rate at which one currency may be exchanged for another for immediate delivery. Currency spot rate is a kind of quoted exchange rate.
Currency spot sell rate is defined as indicative spot selling market rate as observed by the reporting source. Currency spot sell rate is a kind of currency spot rate.
Currency spot volatility is defined as measure of exchange rate fluctuation based on a range of past actual values for exchange rates. Currency spot volatility is a kind of exchange rate volatility, historical volatility. It is volatility of only currency spot rate.
Exchange rate structure is defined as structured collection of quoted or projected exchange rates, such that volatility may be constructed for the structure. Exchange rate structure is a kind of dated structured collection. It has constituent some exchange rate.
Exchange rate volatility is defined as statistical measure of the rate of change in the rate at which one currency can be exchanged for another. Exchange rate volatility is a kind of volatility. It has argument some exchange rate structure.
Foreign exchange service is defined as financial service involving the exchange of one currency for another, conversion of one currency for another, and transfer of money from one country to another whereby currency conversion is required. Foreign exchange service is a kind of financial service.
International electronic funds transfer service is defined as electronic funds transfer (EFT) service involving the transfer of funds across national borders, that may also involve currency conversion. International electronic funds transfer service has synonyms international wire transfer. International electronic funds transfer service is a kind of electronic funds transfer service, foreign exchange service.
Quoted exchange rate is defined as exchange rate quoted at a specific point in time, for a given block amount of currency as quoted against another (base) currency. Quoted exchange rate is a kind of exchange rate, market rate. It has quote currency exactly one currency.
