Base rate is defined as basic rate of interest on which the actual rate a bank charges on loans to its customers is calculated. Base rate has synonyms bank base rate. Base rate is a kind of reference interest rate.
Interbank bid rate is defined as interbank rate that is the interest rate at which participating banks are willing to borrow deposits from other banks. Interbank bid rate is a kind of interbank rate.
Interbank mid rate is defined as interbank rate that represents the mid-point between bid and offer rates. Interbank mid rate is a kind of interbank rate.
Interbank offered rate is defined as interbank rate that is the interest rate at which participating banks lend money. Interbank offered rate is a kind of interbank rate.
Interbank rate is defined as reference rate that is the rate of interest charged on short-term loans between banks. Interbank rate is a kind of reference interest rate.
Interest rate authority is defined as financial service provider/publisher responsible for specifying some benchmark interest rate. Interest rate authority has examples This is typically a bank, central bank in the case of the publication of bank interest rates, or the committee responsible for publishing interbank rates, such as EURIBOR.. Interest rate authority is a kind of financial service provider, publisher. It produces some reference interest rate.
Interest rate benchmark is defined as classifier for regularly updated interest rates that are publicly accessible, typically set by a central bank or group of financial institutions. Interest rate benchmark is a kind of quantity kind. It classifies some reference interest rate. It has rate reset time of day at least zero date time. It has reference currency exactly one currency. It has tenor at least zero duration. It is produced by at least zero publisher. It is provided by at least zero market data provider.
Interest rate benchmark classification scheme is defined as scheme for classifying interest rate benchmarks, such as the FpML classification scheme. Interest rate benchmark classification scheme is a kind of classification scheme. It defines some interest rate benchmark.
Overnight rate is defined as reference rate that is an interest rate at which a depository institution lends funds to another depository institution (short-term), or the interest rate the central bank charges a financial institution to borrow money overnight. Overnight rate is a kind of reference interest rate. It has tenor value one day.
Reference interest rate is defined as market rate that is a rate of interest paid by or agreed among some bank or set of banks. Reference interest rate is a kind of interest rate, market rate. It has quantity kind some interest rate benchmark. It has rate reset time of day at least zero date time. It has reference currency exactly one currency. It has tenor only duration.
Specific-provider interest rate benchmark is defined as interest rate benchmark that is made available by a specific market data provider for reference purposes. Specific-provider interest rate benchmark is a kind of interest rate benchmark. It is provided by exactly one market data provider.
