
Board agreement is defined as formal, legally binding agreement between members of the Board of Directors of the organization. Board agreement is a kind of organization covering agreement.
Joint stock company is defined as for-profit, unincorporated business that has some characteristics of a corporation and some features of a partnership, with ownership interests represented by shares of stock. Joint stock company is a kind of business entity.
Privately held company is defined as corporation whose issued shares are all held by a family or a small group of investors and, therefore, cannot be bought by the public. Privately held company has synonyms closed corporation privately held corporation. Privately held company is a kind of stock corporation.
Publicly held company is defined as corporation whose shares are traded and held publicly. Publicly held company is a kind of stock corporation.
Registration identifier is defined as identifier that is officially allocated to an organization at the time of registration, typically in a jurisdiction in which said organization is organized or registered and used in that jurisdiction to identify the organization. Registration identifier is a kind of organization identifier. It identifies exactly one formal organization. It is defined in some registration identifier scheme. It is governed by at least zero jurisdiction.
Registration identifier scheme is defined as scheme that defines the registration identifier per the issuing registration authority. Registration identifier scheme is a kind of organization identification scheme. It defines some registration identifier.
Religious corporation is defined as not-for-profit corporation whose objective is specific to some fundamental set of beliefs and practices generally agreed upon by a number of people, and that is incorporated under the law. Religious corporation is a kind of not-for-profit corporation. It has objective some religious objective.
