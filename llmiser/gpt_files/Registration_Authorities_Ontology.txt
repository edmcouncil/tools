
Registrar is defined as party that has the capacity to act as a representative of a registration authority to provide registration services, including official record keeping. Registrar is a kind of party in role. It is played by some that that has capacity some registration capacity and that is designated by some that plays role some registration authority. It registers some Thing.
Registration authority is defined as service provider that is responsible for maintaining a registry and provides registration services. Registration authority is a kind of service provider. It has identity exactly one legal entity. It is registration authority for some Thing. It manages at least zero registry. It provides at least zero registration service. It registers at least zero Thing.
Registration capacity is defined as the capacity of some natural person to formally register information in a registry on behalf of some registration authority. Registration capacity is a kind of legal capacity.
Registration scheme is defined as scheme for organizing information and allocating identifiers to items in a registry. Registration scheme is a kind of identification scheme. It has member some registry identifier.
Registration service is defined as service provided to individuals and/or organizations to register items in a registry. Registration service is a kind of service. It is provided by some that registrar or registration authority.
Registry is defined as authoritative record or collection of records relating to something. Registry has synonyms register. Registry is a kind of record, structured collection. It has registry entry some registry entry. It is managed by some that registrar or registration authority.
Registry entry is defined as independently identified entry for something recorded in a registry. Registry entry is a kind of dated collection constituent. It comprises some registry identifier. It is constituent of only registry.
Registry identifier is defined as identifier associated with an entry in a registry, i.e., one that provides an index into the registry for the identified item. Registry identifier is a kind of identifier. It is member of exactly one registration scheme. It is registered in some registry.
