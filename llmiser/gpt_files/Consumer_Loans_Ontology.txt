
Consumer loan is defined as loan to one or more individuals for household, family, or other personal expenditures, excluding mortgages and student loans. Consumer loan has examples Other secured consumer loans may include loans against insurance policies, against securities held in a retirement account, loans secured with collateral other than for the loan's stated purpose, lines of credit other than credit card agreements, and so forth.. Consumer loan is a kind of loan. It has some borrower that is that consumer or that is played by some legally competent natural person.
Home equity line of credit is defined as line of credit granted to a homeowner secured by the equity value in a borrower's home or other property. Home equity line of credit is a kind of collateralized loan, revolving line of credit, secured consumer loan. It is collateralized by some physical collateral.
Motor vehicle loan is defined as collateralized, simple-interest loan that is repaid in monthly installments over a period of typically three to five years, for the purpose of purchasing a vehicle. Motor vehicle loan has synonyms auto loan. Motor vehicle loan is a kind of collateralized loan, secured consumer loan. It is collateralized by some physical collateral.
Secured consumer loan is defined as loan to one or more individuals for household, family, or other personal expenditures in which the borrower pledges some asset via a security agreement as collateral for the loan, or that is secured via third-party guarantee. Secured consumer loan is a kind of consumer loan, secured loan.
Unsecured consumer loan is defined as loan to one or more individuals for household, family, or other personal expenditures granted based on the strength of the borrower's credit history or reputation in the community. Unsecured consumer loan is a kind of consumer loan, unsecured loan.
