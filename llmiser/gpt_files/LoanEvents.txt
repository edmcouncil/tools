Court judgment is defined as decision by a court or other tribunal that resolves a controversy and determines the rights and obligations of the parties. Court judgment is a kind of legal proceeding. It has judgement amount at least zero monetary amount. It is delivered by some court of law.
Legal proceeding is defined as legal step or action taken at the direction of, or by the authority of, a court or agency. Legal proceeding is a kind of occurrence. It is against some legal person. It is governed by some jurisdiction.
