
Allotment right is defined as privileges allotted to existing security holders, entitling them to receive new securities free of charge. Allotment right has synonyms bonus right. Allotment right is a kind of entitlement, equity derivative. It has formula some allotment right formula.
Allotment right formula is defined as rule applied to calculate the number of securities for an allotment right, typically based on the number of these instruments that the holder holds. Allotment right formula is a kind of formula.
Barrier instrument-based mini-future certificate is defined as mini-future certificate that immediately expires if the barrier instrument trading price level is breached during product lifetime. Barrier instrument-based mini-future certificate is a kind of mini-future certificate.
Barrier underlying mini-future certificate is defined as mini-future certificate that immediately expires if the barrier underlying level is breached during product lifetime. Barrier underlying mini-future certificate is a kind of mini-future certificate.
Basket warrant is defined as warrant that permits the holder to acquire a package or group of assets at a specified price during a specified period. Basket warrant is a kind of warrant. It has underlier some basket of securities.

Bond purchase right is a kind of purchase right. It has some underlier that is that has identity some bond.
Bond subscription right is defined as subscription right entitling existing bond holders to subscribe to new securities at a price normally lower than the prevailing market price. Bond subscription right is a kind of subscription right. It has some underlier that is that has identity some bond.

Covered warrant is defined as warrant that gives the holder the right, but not the obligation, to buy (call warrant) or to sell (put warrant) an underlying asset at a specified price (the strike or exercise price) by a predetermined date, issued without an accompanying security by a third party that holds as many securities as would be required if all the warrants are exercised. Covered warrant is a kind of warrant. It is issued by some financial institution.

Mini-future certificate is a kind of barrier option, entitlement, security-based derivative.
