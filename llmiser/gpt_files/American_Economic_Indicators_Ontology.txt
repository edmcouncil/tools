
American statistics publisher is defined as statistical information publisher comprised of the government agencies and departments that publish statistical information on behalf of the U.S. Federal Government. American statistics publisher has examples Examples of American statistical information publishers include the Department of Commerce, Bureau of Economic Analysis (Commerce), Census Bureau (Commerce), Bureau of Labor Statistics (Labor), Department of the Treasury, and the Federal Reserve Board, among others.. American statistics publisher is a kind of statistical information publisher.
U.S. producer price index is defined as an economic indicator representing a measure of the change over time in the selling prices received by domestic producers for their output. U.S. producer price index is a kind of producer price index. It has publisher some American statistics publisher. It is published by value Bureau of Labor Statistics.
Consumer expenditure survey is defined as statistical program conducted on a regular basis that provides information on the buying habits of consumers, including data on their expenditures, income, and consumer unit (families and single consumers) characteristics. Consumer expenditure survey is a kind of statistical program.
Current employment statistics is defined as survey conducted on a regular basis that presents analytical information related to businesses and government agencies, in order to provide detailed industry data on employment, hours, and earnings of workers. Current employment statistics is a kind of statistical program.
Current population survey is defined as survey conducted on a regular basis that presents analytical information related to the general population of a given statistical area with respect to labor force, employment, unemployment, persons not in the labor force, hours of work, earnings, and other demographic and labor force characteristics. Current population survey is a kind of statistical program. It specifies some civilian non-institutional population.
Employment situation establishment survey is defined as survey conducted on a regular basis that presents analytical information related to the labor force of a given statistical area, surveyed with respect to businesses, and is, for the most part, seasonally adjusted. Employment situation establishment survey is a kind of employment situation survey. It is applicable in some establishment.
Employment situation household survey is defined as a survey conducted on a regular basis that presents analytical information related to the labor force of a given statistical area, surveyed with respect to households, and is, for the most part, seasonally adjusted. Employment situation household survey is a kind of employment situation survey. It is applicable in some household.
Employment situation survey is defined as a survey conducted on a regular basis that presents analytical information focused on the employment characteristics of a given statistical area. Employment situation survey is a kind of current employment statistics, current population survey. It is seasonally adjusted exactly one boolean.
Point of purchase survey is defined as a program conducted on a regular basis that provides information on purchases of various items and services by consumers. Point of purchase survey is a kind of statistical program.
Urban consumer price index is defined as an economic indicator representing a measure of the average change over time in the prices paid by urban consumers for a market basket of consumer goods and services. Urban consumer price index is a kind of consumer price index. It applies to some urban consumers universe. It has argument exactly one urban consumers universe. It has argument some basket. It has publisher some American statistics publisher. It is published by value Bureau of Labor Statistics.
Urban consumers universe is defined as a statistical universe for consumer expenditure surveys consisting of people within a household that make joint expenditure decisions. Urban consumers universe is a kind of civilian non-institutional population. It has some member that is that plays role some ultimate consumer. It is applicable in some consumer expenditure survey. It is applicable in some point of purchase survey.
