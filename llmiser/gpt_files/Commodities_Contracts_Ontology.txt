
Agricultural resource is defined as negotiable commodity that is obtained via some agricultural process, including forestry, fishing, livestock, grain, dairy, corn, cocoa, soybeans, sugar, coffee. Agricultural resource is a kind of negotiable commodity.
Base metal is defined as common metal that tarnishes, oxidizes, or corrodes relatively quickly when exposed to air or moisture, that is widely used in commercial and industrial applications, such as construction and manufacturing. Base metal is a kind of metal.
Basket of commodities is defined as custom basket whose constituents consist of one or more negotiable commodities. Basket of commodities is a kind of weighted basket. It has constituent some commodity basket constituent.
Bullion is defined as physical precious metal that is officially recognized as being at least 99.5 percent pure. Bullion has examples In the United States, bullion that is eligible for reference in a commodities contract may include U.S. gold Buffalo coins minted by the U.S. Mint that are 1 troy ounce, 0.5 ounce, 0.25 ounce, or 0.10 ounce; 1 ounce silver coins; certain platinum coins; and gold, silver, palladium, and platinum bullion that meet or exceed the fineness requirements of a regulated futures contract. Bullion must also be certified by an approved certifier, typically identified by an exchange, including but not limited to the U.S. Mint.. Bullion is a kind of precious metal.
Commodity basket constituent is defined as component of a custom commodity basket whose relative importance with respect to other basket constituents is known. Commodity basket constituent is a kind of weighted basket constituent. It has as-of date at least zero explicit date. It has commodity value as of date at least zero monetary amount. It has quantity value at least zero quantity value. It involves at least zero commodity.
Commodity derivative is defined as derivative instrument whose primary underlying notional item is a physical commodity, or the price, or related index, or any other aspect related to a physical commodity. Commodity derivative is a kind of commodity instrument, derivative instrument. It has underlier at least zero commodity underlying asset.
Commodity forward is defined as forward contract in which a buyer and seller agree upon delivery of a specified quality and quantity of goods at a specified future date. Commodity forward is a kind of commodity derivative, forward.
Commodity future is defined as futures contract to buy or sell a predetermined amount of a commodity at a specific price on a specific date in the future. Commodity future is a kind of commodity derivative, future.
Commodity option is defined as option where the option buyer has the right to buy or sell specified commodities or commodity related index at a fixed price or formula, on or before a specified date. Commodity option is a kind of commodity derivative, vanilla option.
Commodity return leg is defined as leg of a commodity return swap in which the return is based on a physical commodity, or the price, or behavior of the price, or any other aspect related to a physical commodity. Commodity return leg is a kind of return leg. It has underlier some commodity underlying asset.
Commodity spot contract is defined as contract that involves physical delivery of the commodity asset at settlement. Commodity spot contract is a kind of commodity instrument, spot contract.
Commodity swap is defined as commodity derivative that includes, without limitation, any swap for which the primary underlying notional item is a physical commodity, or the price, or behavior of the price, or the level of a commodity index, or other aspect related to a physical commodity. Commodity swap is a kind of commodity derivative, return swap. It has return leg some commodity return leg.
Commodity underlying asset is defined as underlier of a commodity derivative, including, but not limited to, the negotiable commodity itself. Commodity underlying asset is a kind of underlier. It has as-of date at least zero explicit date. It has commodity value as of date at least zero monetary amount. It has quantity value at least zero quantity value. It involves at least zero commodity.
Energy resource is defined as negotiable commodity that is an energy-related product, or a derivative of an energy-related product, including electricity, renewable energy, or any power/energy delivered through a utility network of provider; diesel fuel, fuel oil, gas oil, gasoline, heating oil, jet fuel, kerosene, natural gas, oil (Brent, Tapis, Dubai, WTI). Energy resource is a kind of negotiable commodity.
Energy transmission rights is defined as rights to the transmission of power across an electricity distribution network. Energy transmission rights is a kind of energy resource.
Environmental resource is defined as negotiable commodity including offset credits. Environmental resource is a kind of negotiable commodity.
Extraction resource is defined as negotiable commodity that is a mineral resource obtained via withdrawal from the natural environment. Extraction resource is a kind of negotiable commodity.
Fertilizer resource is defined as negotiable commodity that includes ammonia, diammonium phosphate (DAP), potash, sulphur, urea, urea and ammonium nitrate (UAN). Fertilizer resource is a kind of negotiable commodity.
Freight resource is defined as negotiable commodity that is a freight index route. Freight resource is a kind of negotiable commodity.
Gas commodity is defined as extraction resource that is natural gas. Gas commodity is a kind of energy resource, extraction resource.
Generated resource is defined as negotiable commodity obtained via some generation process, including electricity, renewable energy, or any power/energy delivered through a utility network or provider. Generated resource is a kind of negotiable commodity.
Grain commodity is defined as agricultural resource that is a small, hard seed, of a food plant, especially of a cereal plant, such as wheat, corn, rye, oats, rice, and millet. Grain commodity is a kind of agricultural resource.
Industrial resource is defined as negotiable commodity that is obtained via some industrial process, including construction and manufacturing. Industrial resource is a kind of negotiable commodity.
Meat commodity is defined as agricultural resource derived from the flesh of an animal (especially a mammal), such as pork bellies. Meat commodity is a kind of agricultural resource.
Metal is defined as material that, when freshly prepared, polished, or fractured, shows a lustrous appearance, and conducts electricity and heat relatively well. Metal has examples Examples include precious or industrial metal, such as aluminium, copper, gold, lead, nickel, platinum, silver, tin, zinc.. Metal is a kind of extraction resource.
Oil commodity is defined as generated resource that is a viscous liquid derived from petroleum, including for use as fuel, or as a lubricant, and the manufacture of many types of paints, plastics, and other materials. Oil commodity is a kind of generated resource. It has grade some oil grade.
Oil grade is defined as measure of the viscosity of oil during operation. Oil grade is a kind of classifier.
Paper resource is defined as negotiable commodity that is a paper product, including containerboard, newsprint, pulp, recovered paper. Paper resource is a kind of negotiable commodity.
Polypropylene resource is defined as negotiable commodity including plastics. Polypropylene resource is a kind of negotiable commodity.
Service resource is defined as negotiable commodity involving services such as transportation, communications, and trade. Service resource is a kind of negotiable commodity.
Weather derivative is defined as derivative instrument whose primary underlying notional item is based on something related to the weather, for example, the average temperature in Chicago in January. Weather derivative is a kind of commodity derivative, derivative instrument.
