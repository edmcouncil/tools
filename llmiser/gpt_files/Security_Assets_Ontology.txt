
Portfolio is defined as a collection of investments (financial assets) such as stocks, bonds and cash equivalents, as well as mutual funds. Portfolio is a kind of collection. It comprises some portfolio holding. It is held by only that investor or financial service provider. It is managed by some that investor or financial service provider.
Portfolio holding is defined as the contents of holding of one or more portfolios of investments held by an individual investor or entity. Portfolio holding is a kind of financial asset. It is held by only that investor or financial service provider. It is part of some portfolio.
