
Collateralized guaranty is defined as guaranty that takes the form of some asset that is pledged by a borrower to a lender (usually in return for a loan). Collateralized guaranty is a kind of guaranty. It is collateralized by some collateral.
Government guaranty is defined as guaranty provided by a government entity, such as for a government-backed security. Government guaranty is a kind of guaranty. It is guaranteed by some that is played by some polity.
Guarantor is defined as party that guarantees, endorses, or provides indemnity for some obligation on behalf of some other party. Guarantor is a kind of contract third party, de jure controlling interest party. It has identity only legal person. It is played by some that is a party to some contract.
Guaranty is defined as commitment whereby something is formally assured if a party with primary liability fails to perform. Guaranty is a kind of commitment. It has expiration date only date. It has guaranteed amount only monetary amount. It has priority level only priority level. It holds during only date period. It is guaranteed by only guarantor.
Insurance policy is defined as contract document that (1) puts an indemnity cover into effect, (2) serves as a legal evidence of the insurance agreement, (3) sets out the exact terms on which the indemnity cover has been provided, and (4) states associated information such as the (a) specific risks and perils covered, (b) duration of coverage, (c) amount of premium, (d) mode of premium payment, and (e) deductibles, if any. Insurance policy is a kind of contract document. It has counterparty some policyholder. It is issued by some insurer.
Insurance-backed guaranty is defined as guaranty that is realized as an insurance policy. Insurance-backed guaranty is a kind of guaranty. It is realized by some insurance policy.
Insurer is defined as financial service provider that issues an insurance policy. Insurer is a kind of contract principal, financial service provider.
Joint guaranty is defined as guaranty provided by at least two parties, jointly and severally. Joint guaranty is a kind of guaranty. It is guaranteed by exactly 2 guarantor.
Letter of credit is defined as letter from a bank or other creditworthy institution guaranteeing that a buyer's payment to a seller will be received on time and for the correct amount. Letter of credit is a kind of committed credit facility, financial asset. It is issued by some party in role.
Letter of credit guaranty is defined as guaranty that takes the form of a letter of credit, i.e., a document issued by a bank guaranteeing the payment up to a stated amount for a specified period. Letter of credit guaranty is a kind of collateralized guaranty. It is realized by some letter of credit.
Negative pledge is defined as guaranty whereby the issuer will not pledge any assets if doing so would result in less security for lender(s) or investor(s). Negative pledge is a kind of guaranty.
Policyholder is defined as counterparty to and typically owner of an insurance policy. Policyholder has synonyms insured party. Policyholder is a kind of counterparty.
Priority level is defined as relative ranking that a guaranty has in the context of a contract, for example for a credit enhancement priority. Priority level is a kind of classifier.
