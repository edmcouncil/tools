Mortgage is defined as a loan contract that is secured by real property. Mortgage is a kind of closed-end credit, collateralized loan. It assumes at least zero mortgage. It has closing date some date. It has guarantor at least zero mortgage indemnity guarantor. It has some originating service provider that is that financial service provider and that is identified by some Nationwide It Licensing System and Registry Identifier. It has some originator person that is that legally competent natural person and that is identified by some Nationwide It Licensing System and Registry Identifier. It is identified by at least zero universal loan identifier. It is performed by at least zero servicer.
Mortgage indemnity guarantee is defined as insurance-backed guarantee that protects lenders if the mortgage repayments cease for some reason, perhaps due to death or a homeowner being made redundant at work. Mortgage indemnity guarantee is a kind of insurance-backed guaranty. It is guaranteed by some mortgage indemnity guarantor. It is realized by some mortgage indemnity insurance policy.
Mortgage indemnity guarantor is defined as guarantor and insurer that provides mortgage insurance in the form of a mortgage indemnity guarantee (MIG). Mortgage indemnity guarantor is a kind of guarantor, insurer.
Mortgage indemnity insurance policy is defined as insurance policy providing the mortgage indemnity guarantee. Mortgage indemnity insurance policy is a kind of insurance policy. It is evidence for some mortgage indemnity guarantee.
Mortgage loan purpose is defined as the purpose for which mortgage loan proceeds will be used, such as real property purchase, dwelling construction, or loan refinancing. Mortgage loan purpose is a kind of classifier, loan purpose.

Reverse mortgage is defined as mortgage contract that pays out money to the borrower against a set principal limit that is based on the value of existing equity in the underlying collateral.. Reverse mortgage is a kind of mortgage. It has credit limit some monetary amount.
