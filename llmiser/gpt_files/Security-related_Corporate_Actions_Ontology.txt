
Bond default event is defined as corporate action which indicates a failure by the issuer to perform obligations defined as default events under the bond agreement and that have not been remedied. Bond default event is a kind of mandatory corporate action, obligation default. It applies to some bond.
Bonus issue is defined as corporate action in which security holders are awarded additional assets free of payment from the issuer in proportion to their holding. Bonus issue has synonyms capitalisation issue  scrip issue. Bonus issue is a kind of mandatory corporate action.
Bonus rights issue is defined as a Rights Issue in which the rights are given to the holders of the referenced shares for free.. Bonus rights issue is a kind of intermediate securities distribution.
Bonus share plan distribution is defined as event in which shares are issued from the Share Premium Reserve of the company and are considered as a capital distribution rather than a disbursement of income, with different tax implications. Call on intermediate securities is defined as corporate action that involves a call or exercise on nil paid securities or intermediate securities resulting from an intermediate securities distribution (RHDI). Call on intermediate securities is a kind of mandatory with choice corporate action.
Cancellation of shares is defined as the cancellation of shares. Further Notes Only possible with shares not publicly issued i.e. treasury shares. Cancellation of shares is a kind of corporate action.
Capital distribution is defined as corporate action that pays shareholders an amount in cash issued from the issuer's capital account. Capital distribution is a kind of voluntary corporate action.
Capital gains distribution is defined as corporate action that distributes profits resulting from the sale of company assets to shareholders. Capital gains distribution has examples Shareholders of Mutual Funds, Unit Trusts, or SICAVs are recipients of capital gains distributions which are often reinvested in additional shares of the fund. Capital gains distribution is a kind of voluntary corporate action.
Cash dividend action is defined as corporate action that distributes cash to shareholders in proportion to their equity holding. Cash dividend action is a kind of mandatory corporate action.
Change of security trading status event is defined as an event in which the trading status of a tradable security changes.. Change of security trading status event is a kind of corporate change of status event. It applies to some security trading status.
Change to smallest negotiable unit is defined as modification of the smallest negotiable unit of shares in order to obtain a new negotiable unit. SWIFT:SMAL. Change to smallest negotiable unit is a kind of corporate action.
Conversion action is defined as corporate action involving conversion of securities (generally convertible bonds or preferred shares) into another form of securities (usually common shares) at a pre-stated price/ratio. Conversion action is a kind of voluntary corporate action. It converts from some security. It converts to some security.
Conversion suspension action is defined as suspension of conversion of securities, generally a couple of weeks before a meeting takes place. SWIFT = SCOP. Conversion suspension action is a kind of corporate action.
Corporate action obligation is defined as an obligation related to the holding of a Security.. Corporate action obligation is a kind of corporate action, duty.
 Corporate action payment obligation is a kind of corporate action obligation.

Corporate change of status event is defined as some change to the status of some security.. Corporate change of status event is a kind of corporate action, lifecycle event.
Coupon strip is defined as coupon stripping is the process whereby interest coupons for future payment dates are separated from the security corpus that entitles the holder to the principal repayment.. Coupon strip is a kind of corporate action. It applies to some bond.
Decrease in value action is defined as corporate action resulting in a reduction of face value of a share or the value of fund assets. Decrease in value action is a kind of mandatory corporate action.
Dividend option action is defined as corporate action that involves distribution of a dividend to shareholders with a choice of benefit to receive. Dividend option action is a kind of mandatory with choice corporate action.
Dividend reinvestment action is defined as corporate action that involves a dividend payment whereby holders can keep cash or have the cash reinvested into additional shares in the issuing company. Dividend reinvestment action is a kind of mandatory with choice corporate action.
Drawing is defined as redemption in part before the scheduled final maturity date of a security.. Drawing is a kind of mandatory corporate action.
Dutch auction is defined as corporate action by a party wishing to acquire a security. Dutch auction is a kind of voluntary corporate action.
Exchange action is defined as corporate action that reflects an exchange of holdings for other securities and/or cash. Exchange action is a kind of corporate action.
Formal offer is defined as corporate action involving an offer to a holder, potential holder, or other intersted party, to enter into a trade which is or would be legally binding on the part of the party making the offer (the offeror).. Formal offer is a kind of voluntary corporate action.
Full call early redemption action is defined as corporate action that involves redemption of an entire issue outstanding of securities prior to maturity. Full call early redemption action has examples Examples securities that may be subject to a full call/early redemption include bonds, preferred equity, funds, that may be redeemed by the issuer or its agent before final maturity.. Full call early redemption action is a kind of mandatory corporate action.
Interest payment action is defined as corporate action that involves distribution of a regular interest payment to holders of an interest-bearing asset. Interest payment action is a kind of mandatory corporate action.
Interest payment in kind is defined as interest payment, in any kind except cash, distributed to holders of an interest bearing asset. SWIFT = PINK. Interest payment in kind is a kind of interest payment action.
Interest payment with principal is defined as a payment of a portion of the principal of an interest bearing asset, in addition to the interest payment. SWIFT = PRII. Interest payment with principal is a kind of interest payment action.
Interest rate adjustment is defined as scheduled change to the coupon rate for a floating or adjustable rate security.. Interest rate adjustment is a kind of mandatory corporate action. It applies to some variable coupon bond.
Intermediate securities distribution is defined as corporate action involving the distribution of intermediate securities or privilege that gives the holder the right to take part in a future event. Intermediate securities distribution has synonyms rights distribution. Intermediate securities distribution is a kind of mandatory corporate action.
Listing status delisting message is defined as security is no longer able to comply with the listing requirements of a stock exchange and is removed from official board quotation.. Listing status delisting message is a kind of notice.
Odd lot offer is defined as offer by issuer to allow holders of an odd lot of a security to order a commission-free transaction at market price, to sell the odd lot, or to buy an amount of shares which will bring the position to a round lot (board lot). SWIFT = ODLT. Odd lot offer is a kind of voluntary corporate action.
Pari-passu action is defined as corporate action that occurs when securities with different characteristics become identical in all respects. Pari-passu action has synonyms assimilation. Pari-passu action is a kind of mandatory corporate action.
Partial redemption with reduction of nominal value action is defined as corporate action that involves redemption of securities in part before their scheduled final maturity date with reduction of the nominal value of the securities. Partial redemption with reduction of nominal value action is a kind of corporate action.
Partial redemption without reduction of nominal value action is defined as corporate action that involves redemption of securities in part before their scheduled final maturity date with no reduction in nominal value. Partial redemption without reduction of nominal value action is a kind of corporate action.
Post-merger securities exchange is defined as corporate action that involves the exchange of outstanding securities, initiated by the issuer which may include options, as the result of two or more companies combining assets, that is, an external, third party company. Post-merger securities exchange is a kind of corporate action. It converts from some security. It converts to some security. It has monetary amount at least zero monetary amount.
Put redemption action is defined as corporate action involving early redemption of a security at the election of the holder subject to the terms and condition of the issue with no reduction in nominal value. Put redemption action is a kind of voluntary corporate action.
Redemption at maturity action is defined as corporate action that involves redemption of an entire issue outstanding of securities at final maturity. Redemption at maturity action is a kind of mandatory corporate action.
Redenomination action is defined as corporate action by which the unit (currency and/or nominal) of a security is restated. Redenomination action is a kind of mandatory corporate action.
Repurchase offer is defined as corporate action in which an offer is made to existing shareholders by the issuing company to repurchase equity or other securities convertible into equity. Repurchase offer has synonyms issuer bid  reverse rights. Repurchase offer is a kind of voluntary corporate action.
Reverse stock split is defined as corporate action involving a decrease in a company's number of outstanding equities without any change in the shareholder's equity or the aggregate market value at the time of the split. Reverse stock split has synonyms change in nominal value. Reverse stock split is a kind of mandatory corporate action. It applies to some share.
Rights exercise event is defined as exercising the right to purchase the shares. Furhter Notes: This is an action on the part of the holder. SWIFT: Call/exercise on nil-paid securities/rights resulting from a rights distribution (RHDI) (To be used for the second event in case rights issue is dealt with in 2 events, first event being the RHDI).. Rights exercise event is a kind of corporate action.
Shares premium dividend action is defined as corporate action that pays shareholders an amount in cash issued from the shares premium reserve. Shares premium dividend action is a kind of mandatory with choice corporate action.
Stock dividend action is defined as corporate action that involves distribution of a dividend to shareholders in the form of equities of the issuing corporation. Stock dividend action is a kind of mandatory corporate action.
Stock split is defined as corporate action involving an increase in a corporation's number of outstanding equities without any change in the shareholder's equity or the aggregate market value at the time of the split. Stock split has synonyms change in nominal value  subdivision. Stock split is a kind of mandatory corporate action. It applies to some share.
Tender offer is defined as corporate action involving an offer made to shareholders, normally by a third party, requesting them to sell (tender) or exchange their equities. Tender offer has synonyms acquisition  buyback  purchase offer  takeover. Tender offer is a kind of voluntary corporate action. It applies to some share.
Trading status active message is defined as trading in security has commenced or security has been re-activated after a supsension in trading.. Trading status active message is a kind of trading status message.
Trading status message is defined as a message about trading status.. Trading status message is a kind of notice. It is about some security trading status. It refers to some security.
Trading status suspended message is defined as trading in the security has been suspended.. Trading status suspended message is a kind of trading status message.
Warrant exercise action is defined as corporate action that offers holders the option to buy (call warrant) or to sell (put warrant) a specific amount of stock, cash, or commodity, at a predetermined price, during a predetermined period of time (which usually corresponds to the life of the issue). Warrant exercise action is a kind of corporate action.
Worthless security action is defined as corporate action that involves booking out of valueless securities. Worthless security action is a kind of mandatory corporate action.
